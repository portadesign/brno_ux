SOS · I need help now

If you’re in immediate danger, call 112 — the free EU-wide emergency line works in any EU language. This page collects the most-needed crisis contacts in Brno: lost documents, accommodation, violence, mental health, addiction, financial trouble and embassy help.

Emergency phone numbers

112 is free and connects to an operator who speaks any major EU language. Other lines are Czech-only — try 112 first if you can’t communicate in Czech.

Language barrier?

112 operates in any major EU language. For other lines, ask for an interpreter — police and ambulance have on-call services.
