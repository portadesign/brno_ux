Detail tématu (L3)

Brno's integrated transport system (IDS JMK) connects the city and South Moravia — trams, buses, trolleybuses, regional trains and on-demand services. As a foreign resident you'll likely use the public network daily, register a vehicle, or apply for a resident parking permit.

About transport in Brno

The Department of Transport manages city roads, traffic regulation, parking permits and vehicle administration. IDS JMK is the integrated ticketing system covering the whole region.

  • Public transport hours: 4:30–23:30 daily; night service on selected routes.
  • Annual pass: ~5,000 CZK; monthly ~700 CZK; single ticket ~30 CZK.
  • Resident parking zones cover most central districts — registration via city portal.
  • Vehicle registration requires technical inspection (STK) + insurance proof.
  • Driver's licence: EU licences valid up to 5 years; non-EU need to exchange within 3–12 months.
