About transport in Brno

Brno's integrated transport system (IDS JMK) connects the city and the South Moravian region with trams, buses, trolleybuses, regional trains and on-demand services. As a foreign resident you'll likely use the public network daily, register a vehicle, or apply for a resident parking permit.

  • Operates and develops Brno's integrated transport system (IDS JMK).
  • Issues parking permits and disabled parking spaces.
  • Handles vehicle registration, change of owner, deregistration and import.
  • Maintains city roads, bridges, signage and bike infrastructure.
  • Coordinates road closures, major works and traffic regulations.
