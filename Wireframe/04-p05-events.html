What's happening in the city — calendars, festivals, trade fairs and community events. Brno has events nearly every weekend: from massive trade fairs at the BVV exhibition centre to intimate language meetups at the Expat Centre.

About events in Brno

The city's event calendar mixes large-scale international fairs, music festivals, and a year-round expat-friendly meetup scene. Most major events are publicized in English; weekly highlights appear on TIC BRNO and the gotobrno.cz portal.

  • Major venues: BVV exhibition centre (largest in CZ), Sono Centrum, Špilberk Castle outdoor stage.
  • Year-round festivals: Janáček Brno (autumn), Easter & Christmas markets, MotoGP weekend (August).
  • Expat community: Monthly Friday Coffee, Internations meetup, language tandem nights.
  • Family events: Children's Day at Špilberk, Brno Open Days, science weekends at VIDA!
  • Free events: Open-air cinema (summer), Brno Music Marathon, public lectures at MUNI & VUT.
