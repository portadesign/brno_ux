Detail tématu (L3)

Brno is the second-largest research hub in the Czech Republic — home to CEITEC, ICRC, IT4I and active R&D collaboration with five universities. From life sciences to supercomputing, Brno’s research output punches well above the city’s size.

About research in Brno

Three major research centres anchor the city’s science landscape. CEITEC works in nano- & biotech; ICRC runs international clinical trials; IT4I operates one of CEE’s largest supercomputers. JIC mentors spinoffs into industry.

  • CEITEC — Central European Institute of Technology; ~750 researchers in life sciences, materials and nanotech.
  • ICRC — International Clinical Research Center; partner with Mayo Clinic; cardiovascular & neuroscience focus.
  • IT4I — National supercomputing centre at VUT/VŠB; top-500 ranked Karolina cluster.
  • JIC coordinates technology transfer, IP licensing and spinoff support.
  • EURAXESS Brno — EU-wide service helping incoming researchers with visas, housing and family integration.
