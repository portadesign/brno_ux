Detail tématu (L3)

Find your tribe — clubs, exchanges and centres that connect newcomers and longtime residents. Brno's expat scene is friendly and well-organised: regular meetups, nationality-based clubs, activity groups, language exchanges and volunteer opportunities.

About community life in Brno

With 80,000+ students and 30,000+ foreigners working in town, Brno has a constant rotation of newcomers — and a mature welcoming infrastructure. Most communities have English-friendly channels (Facebook groups, Discord, regular in-person events).

  • Brno Expat Centre coordinates city-level newcomer integration — free events, courses, advice.
  • Internations Brno — global expat network with monthly events at upscale venues.
  • National clubs: American Club Brno, Ukrainian House, Spanish-speaking community, Brno Italians.
  • Activity groups: Brno Board Games, Brno Hiking, photography meetups, book clubs.
  • Volunteering & mentoring: Brno Volunteer Centre matches you with NGOs and city projects.
