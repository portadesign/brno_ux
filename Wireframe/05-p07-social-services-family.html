Detail tématu (L3)

Social work, family allowances and disability support from Brno's Social Services Department (OSP). School enrollment for children, foster care, crisis accommodation. Most family allowances apply through Úřad práce, not the city.

About city social services

OSP (Odbor sociální péče) at Koliště 19 is Brno's social work hub. They handle vulnerable groups, crisis intervention, single-parent housing, foster care and disability services. State family allowances go through Úřad práce instead.

  • OSP Brno — Koliště 19; outreach, social work, crisis support.
  • School enrollment — primary school in early April; nursery in early May.
  • Family allowances — applied at Úřad práce, not the city.
  • Crisis accommodation — secret addresses for victims, OSP referral.
  • Disability benefits — combined OSP & Úřad práce flow.
