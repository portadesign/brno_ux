Orient yourself — get around the city, find offices and explore accessible routes. From the public transport map to themed walking tours and the accessible Brno overlay, the city's open-data team publishes maps for nearly every situation.

About city maps in Brno

Brno publishes detailed open-data maps for transport, cycling, accessibility, parking zones and cultural sites. Apps like Mapy.cz and Google Maps cover all city services; printed walking maps are free at TIC BRNO.

  • City portal map: brnomesto.cz/mapa — combine layers (transport, parking, services).
  • Public transport: DPMB map + IDS JMK regional integrated network.
  • Cycling: ~370 km of cycling routes, downloadable GPX tracks.
  • Accessibility: "Brno bez bariér" map shows step-free routes, accessible toilets, entrances.
  • Walking tours: 12+ themed routes — Functionalism, Communism, Underground Brno.
