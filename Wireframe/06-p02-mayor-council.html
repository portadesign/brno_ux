About Brno’s governance

Brno operates a two-tier elected system: an Assembly (55 members, legislative) and a Council (11 members, executive). The Mayor (Primátor) is elected by the Assembly, not directly. Elections happen every 4 years; the next is in autumn 2026.

  • Assembly (Zastupitelstvo) — 55 elected members; legislative body; meets ~monthly.
  • Council (Rada) — 11 members (Mayor + 10 deputies + members); executive body; meets weekly.
  • Mayor (Primátor) — elected from Assembly; current term 2022–2026.
  • Deputy Mayors — portfolios: transport, education, culture, economy, social, environment.
  • Districts — Brno has 29 districts with their own mini-councils for local matters.
  • Open meetings: Assembly meetings are public and livestreamed.
