Brno is one of Central Europe’s biggest student cities — five universities, 80,000+ students, English-language programmes and a vibrant international community. From short-term Erasmus to full PhD, it’s a city built for studying.

About studying in Brno

Brno hosts five public universities offering programmes across all disciplines. International students make up around 13% of the population. English-language degrees span business, medicine, engineering, computer science and arts.

  • Masaryk University (MUNI) — second-largest in CZ, 35,000+ students; strong in medicine, law, IT, social sciences.
  • Brno University of Technology (VUT) — engineering, architecture, CS, fine arts; 22,000+ students.
  • Mendel University (MENDELU) — agriculture, forestry, business, regional development.
  • JAMU — Academy of Performing Arts (music, theatre, film).
  • VFU — University of Veterinary & Pharmaceutical Sciences (one of two in CZ).
  • Tuition: Free for Czech-language programmes; English-language ~3,000–12,000 EUR/year.
