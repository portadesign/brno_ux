About city governance services

City Hall offers two channels: formal civic engagement (petitions, info requests, complaints) and administrative agendas (city honors, logo permissions, employment). Most are governed by national laws (e.g., Act 106/1999 for information requests).

  • City Hall — Dominikánské nám. 196/1; main reception & Assembly meeting hall.
  • Petitions — 1,000+ signatures required for Assembly discussion.
  • Information requests (Act 106) — 15-day legal deadline for response.
  • Complaints & appeals — file in writing or in person at City Hall.
  • City employment — open positions on brno.cz/volna-mista.
