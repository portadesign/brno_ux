Detail tématu (L3)

Brno's labour market is strong in IT, R&D, biotech, automotive and shared-services. Major employers actively recruit English-speaking talent and unemployment for skilled workers stays under 4%.

About Brno's labour market

Brno hosts the second-largest concentration of IT and R&D jobs in Czechia. International companies have English as a working language; smaller Czech firms typically prefer Czech speakers, though tech roles are increasingly an exception.

  • Major employers: Honeywell, Red Hat, Kyndryl, IBM, AT&T, Avast, Y Soft, FEI/Thermo Fisher.
  • Strong sectors: IT & R&D, shared-services, biotech (CEITEC, ICRC), automotive, life sciences.
  • Salary range (2025): Junior tech 50–70k CZK gross, senior 100–180k+, lead/architect 150–250k+.
  • Mobility: Average tenure 3–4 years in tech; salary growth ~5–8% per year.
