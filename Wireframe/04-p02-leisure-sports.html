Active life in Brno — sports clubs, gyms and outdoor pursuits for every season. From football and ice hockey to climbing, hiking around Brno Reservoir, and exploring the Moravian Karst caves, the city offers an outsized variety of options for an EU mid-size capital.

About sports & leisure in Brno

Brno's sports infrastructure spans top-flight professional teams to grassroots community clubs. The city's location between forested hills and the Moravian wine region also makes it a strong base for outdoor recreation year-round.

  • Professional teams: Kometa Brno (ice hockey), FC Zbrojovka (football), Žabiny (basketball), Brno Sigma (volleyball).
  • Public sport facilities: 7 swimming pools, 3 ice rinks, climbing gyms, tennis courts, athletic stadiums.
  • Outdoor highlights: Brno Reservoir (swimming, sailing), Moravian Karst, Pálava wine hills, Vysočina mountains.
  • Major sport events: MotoGP at Brno Circuit, Velka Pardubicka (regional), Brno Marathon, hockey playoffs.
  • Affordable access: Most facilities accept ISIC, city benefits cards, and multi-sport passes (Multisport, ActivePass).
