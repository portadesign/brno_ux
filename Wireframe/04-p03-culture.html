Theatres, cinemas, museums, libraries and creative spaces — Brno's vibrant cultural scene for residents and visitors. Two state theatres, dozens of independent venues, and one of the strongest experimental scenes in Central Europe.

About culture in Brno

Brno's cultural identity blends Czech-language tradition with international audiences. State institutions cover opera, ballet, drama and orchestral music; the independent scene drives contemporary theatre, design and arts. Most venues offer English programmes or surtitles.

  • State theatres: Janáček Theatre (opera/ballet), Mahen Theatre (drama), Reduta Theatre (chamber productions).
  • Major museums: Moravian Gallery (5 buildings), Technical Museum, City Museum at Špilberk Castle.
  • Music venues: Janáček Philharmonic, Sono Centrum, Fléda, Stará Pekárna, summer open-air festivals.
  • Cinemas: Lucerna (oldest), Art Cinema, Scala, Cinema City multiplex.
  • Libraries: Moravian Library (one of CZ's largest), Jiří Mahen Library (municipal), university libraries.
