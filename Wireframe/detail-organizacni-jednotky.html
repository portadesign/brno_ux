Department agenda

What this department is responsible for in the city's administrative structure.

  • Local road network — strategic planning, maintenance and investment for streets and infrastructure owned by the city.
  • Traffic organisation — placement of road signs, signals, parking zones, pedestrian crossings and cycling lanes.
  • Parking management — resident parking, paid zones, reserved spots and special permits for historic centre.
  • Public & commercial transport — coordination with DPMB (city transport operator), taxi licensing, road haulage permits.
