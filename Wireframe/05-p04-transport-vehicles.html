Detail tématu (L3)

Vehicle registration, change of owner, import from abroad — Brno's Department of Transport handles all vehicle administrative agendas. Foreigners moving to Czechia often need to register their existing vehicle within 30 days of import.

About vehicle administration

The Department of Transport (Odbor dopravy) at Kounicova 67 is the central hub. Most agendas need an appointment — book online to avoid hours of waiting. Vehicle registration requires technical inspection (STK), insurance proof and proof of ownership.

  • Department of Transport — Kounicova 67, Mo–Fr 8:00–17:00 (Tue & Thu by appointment only)
  • Vehicle registration — needs STK technical inspection + green insurance card + proof of ownership.
  • Import from abroad — extra step: certificate of conformity (COC) or homologation.
  • Plates — issued same day with registration; replacements within ~2 weeks.
  • Parking — resident permits per district; disabled spots assigned individually.
