Detail tématu (L3)

Visa and residence permits are issued by Foreign Police and the Ministry of Interior — not the city. Brno's role is address registration, citizen-register lookups and information services for foreigners settling here.

About residence in Brno

Long-term and permanent residence permits, employee/blue/ICT cards — all go through Foreign Police (Cejl 62b in Brno) and OAMP MOI. The city offers address registration for residents and end-of-residence reporting. For first arrivals, the Brno Expat Centre is the easiest contact.

  • Foreign Police Brno — Cejl 62b · Mo, We 7:30–11:00 & 12:00–14:30
  • Long-term residence permit — for stays over 90 days; requires visa first.
  • Permanent residence — after 5 years of continuous long-term stay.
  • Address registration — your right; required for many other agendas.
  • End-of-residence reporting — important when leaving Czechia permanently.
