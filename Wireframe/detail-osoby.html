Ing. Bc. Pavel Pospíšek

Head of Transport Department · Vedoucí odboru

Transport Department (Odbor dopravy) · Section 4 — Strategy

Pavel Pospíšek leads the Transport Department at Brno City Hall, overseeing the city's road network, traffic regulation, parking permits, vehicle administration and taxi licensing. He represents the department in coordination with DPMB and the Regional Authority of South Moravia.

Agenda & portfolio

Areas the department head is directly responsible for at the Magistrát level.

  • Local road network — strategic planning, maintenance and investment for streets and infrastructure owned by the city.
  • Traffic organisation — placement of road signs, signals, parking zones, pedestrian crossings and cycling lanes.
  • Parking management — resident parking, paid zones, reserved spots and special permits for historic centre.
  • Public & commercial transport — coordination with DPMB, taxi licensing, road haulage permits.
  • Departmental management — 5 subordinate divisions, ~75 staff.

Current projects

Active priorities and initiatives for 2026.

Resident parking — new districts rollout 2026› Cycling backbone — phase 3 connections› Traffic-signal modernisation programme› Brno in Motion — strategic transport vision›
