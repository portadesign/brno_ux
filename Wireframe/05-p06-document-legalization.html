Detail tématu (L3)

Czech POINT is the universal counter for document copies and verifications. Vidimation = copy verification; legalization = signature verification; higher verification (apostille) makes Czech documents usable abroad — and vice versa.

About verification & legalization

Three levels: vidimace (copy is identical to original), legalizace (signature is genuine), and vyšší ověření (higher verification — apostille for international use). All three are routine services at Czech POINT counters; higher verification needs matrika or notary.

  • Vidimation (copy verification) — ~30 CZK per page; on the spot at Czech POINT.
  • Legalization (signature verification) — ~30 CZK per signature; need to sign in front of the clerk.
  • Higher verification (apostille) — for using Czech documents abroad in Hague Convention countries.
  • Czech POINT — universal counter at most Magistrát desks, no appointment needed.
  • Translation — separate service; certified translators listed on justice.cz.
