Apply for an ID card

A valid Czech ID card (občanský průkaz, OP) must be held by every Czech citizen aged 15 or older with permanent residence in the Czech Republic.

An ID card may also be issued to citizens under 15, or to citizens without permanent residence in the country.

An ID card may be issued to a citizen whose legal capacity has been restricted.

An ID card is most often requested due to:

  • issuing an ID card for a child under 15
  • turning 15 (first ID card)
  • expiry of the existing ID card
  • changes in personal data, especially permanent address, surname, or marital status

When applying, you do not fill out a paper form — the clerk records all details on-site, takes your photo and fingerprints.

What you need to bring

  • For first ID at 15: birth certificate and the legal guardian's ID
  • Renewal: existing ID card
  • Change of details: existing ID and supporting documents

Fees

  • Standard issue (30 days), age 15+: free of charge
  • Standard issue (30 days), under 15: CZK 100
  • Express issue (5 working days), age 15+: CZK 500
  • 24-hour express: CZK 1,000

Legislation

Sections 8 to 21 of Act No. 269/2021 Coll., on ID cards.
